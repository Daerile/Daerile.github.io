Characters

When the game launched in 2015, there were 37 characters in the game, but with time, DLC-s started to come out, and the number rose to 51. On this page, you can find the characters that we made a guide for, and if you click their picture, you will get redirected to the appropriate website. Right now we have a guide for 3 characters, Steve Fox, Jin Kazama and Kazuya Mishima. Steve is a boxer who uses weaving and ducking to his advantage, Jin is an expert martial artist and Kazuya is using the Mishima style to fight off his enemies.
