Kazuya

Kazuya Mishima is the son of the Mishima Zaibatsu multinational corporation CEO, Heihachi Mishima. He has a somewhat ambiguous relationship with his father, who threw him off a cliff as a child to train survival skills.

Story

Kazuya Mishima is one of the essential characters in Tekken. He is a muscular young man who had a difficult childhood. Kazuya tried to do everything to prove his strength to his father. These attempts gave rise to many conflicts between them. As a playable character, Kazuya Mishima has two appearances and is quite strong. However, he is still inferior to Akuma and some other characters. He is a mid-range character with relatively strong blows. When he first appeared in the Tekken series of games, many asked that this character must be weakened. Kazuya has many good sides. He did not tolerate the murder of his mother and how he could try to be different in authoritarianism from his father. However, perhaps he could have done better than he did.

Playstyle

A simple character, Kazuya is a standout in the series. Although he has a move set that focuses on powerful single strikes, he can inflict much raw damage with them. To get the most out of Kazuya's moves, he relies heavily on punishing opponents with blocks rather than surprises. It is, in fact, Kazuya's most significant asset that he has one of, if not the best, punishers in every game that he is used. As a whole, he is best used as a defensive character, and mastering his timing is essential if he is to be played long-term.
