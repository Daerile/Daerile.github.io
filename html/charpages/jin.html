Jin

Jin Kazama is one of several central characters in the Tekken-series. He fights both the devil's attempts to take over his mind and the Mishima Zaibatsu corporation (and family) concerned with world domination. Just have pity on him. Who is more dangerous - the enemies outside or the enemy inside?

Story

Jin Kazama was born after the second King of Iron Fist Tournament. Obviously, before that, his parents Jun Kazama and Kazuya Mishima met and fell in love. Little Jin was born in a peaceful mountainous area. There, his mother taught him the fighting style of the Kazama family. Jin Kazama was a talented and kind child. His mother taught him how to control his emotions, think with his head, and feel with his heart from early childhood.

Playstyle

Jin appeared in Tekken 3 and Tekken Tag Tournament as a Mishima-style fighter; however, in reality, Jin's moveset was a hybrid of Kazuya and Jun Kazama's, with many of Jun's moves retained alongside improved versions of Kazuya's moves. Jin's fighting style changed in Tekken 4, while comparable to the Mishima's, Jin lacks their powerful single moves, having to rely on a variety of different tools like superior poking, evasive maneuvers, stealthy stance transitions, and outstanding counter ability which more than make up for his lack of raw damage. His pokes are quick and difficult to read, and none of them are particularly punishable on block, allowing him to maintain constant pressure on his opponent.
